Passionate about creating functional and innovative solutions that connect concept, material and experience.

When I start a project, something usually bugs me first, a product that doesn't hold up, a solution that solved the wrong problem, a form that exists for attention rather than reason. That friction is where I begin.

I design with conviction. When I commit to a project I go deep, into the material logic, the use scenario, the thing it needs to communicate without saying it. I see design as a structured system where identity, material, production, and intention must align. And I think it should contribute clarity, not noise.

What frustrates me most about design today is how often the market comes first and the problem second. Form is not decoration, but consequence. Design is not a reaction to trends, it is a position.

Outside the studio: water, movement, art, music, and videogames.
